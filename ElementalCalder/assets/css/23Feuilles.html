Alexander Calder (American, 1898 - 1976) revolutionized sculpture by inviting unseen forces of the natural world to participate in his art. In SFMoMA's 'Elemental Calder' exhibition, his works manifest the ways the elements - including the movement of the sea and the blowing of the wind - act as both the subject matter and inspiration for many of Calder's Mobiles.
